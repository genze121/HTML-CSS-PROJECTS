London

London is the capital and most populous city of England and the United Kingdom. Standing at 1,398,244 square miles, it is the most populous city in the European Union by area.

Paris

Paris, officially the City of Light, is the capital and most populous city of France. With an estimated 2,229,875 inhabitants in 2018, it is the most populous urban area in the world.

Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is the most populous metropolitan area in the world, with an estimated 37,253,952 inhabitants in 2019.
