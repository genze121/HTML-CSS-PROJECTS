London

London is the capital city of England and the most populous city in the United Kingdom. Standing at 1,356 million, it is the most populous urban area in the world.

Paris

Paris, officially the City of Light, is the capital and most populous city of France, with an estimated 2,140,426 inhabitants as of 2019.

Tokyo

Tokyo, officially the Greater Tokyo Area (GTOA), is the capital and most populous metropolitan area in Japan. With a population of over 13.7 million people, it is the world's most populous major urban area.
